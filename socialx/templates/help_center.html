How can we help you?

You can change your password by going to Menu > Settings > Account and clicking the "Change Password" button.

To make your account private, navigate to Menu > Settings > Privacy & Security and toggle the "Private Account" switch.

To report content, click the three dots (...) on a post or profile and select the "Report" option. Our moderation team will review it as soon as possible.
